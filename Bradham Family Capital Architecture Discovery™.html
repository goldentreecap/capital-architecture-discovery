    Welcome, Jessica and Cole.

    Before we talk about investments, taxes, real estate, or future opportunities, this discovery experience is designed to understand the life this money needs to support.

    Purpose of This Discovery

    This Capital Architecture Discovery™ helps Erik understand both of your perspectives before making recommendations. The goal is to identify what matters most to your family, where you naturally agree, where you may see things differently, and how future capital can be organized to support security, flexibility, income, family priorities, and long-term legacy.

    This is not a test, and there are no wrong answers. Some questions are answered individually by Cole and Jessica because your priorities may not be identical, and that is completely normal. The purpose is to build a balanced strategy that honors both perspectives.

    Time Commitment

    Please set aside at least 25 to 45 minutes to complete this thoughtfully. You do not need perfect answers. Honest answers are much more valuable than polished answers.

    Try to answer based on how you genuinely feel today, not what you think the answer is “supposed” to be.

    How Your Answers Will Be Used

    Your responses will help create a summary of findings that Jessica, Cole, and Erik can review together. The summary is intended to make the next conversation more productive, personal, and balanced.

    The answers are not used to make automatic investment decisions. They are used to guide discussion, education, planning, and future recommendations.

    Privacy & Confidentiality

    The information you provide is intended solely for confidential planning discussions with Golden Tree Wealth Partners and Erik Cascio. Please answer openly and honestly so the recommendations can reflect your real priorities, concerns, and goals.

    This discovery may include personal financial information, family priorities, lifestyle preferences, and planning concerns. These responses should be treated as confidential and used only for the purpose of preparing your Capital Architecture Blueprint and related planning conversations.

    Important disclosure: This discovery tool is for educational, planning, and discussion purposes. It does not replace legal, tax, insurance, or investment advice from the appropriate licensed professionals. Final recommendations should be reviewed with the appropriate advisors before implementation.

    Important: The current financial snapshot does not include Cole's future company-sale proceeds. This process is designed to prepare the architecture before the liquidity event occurs.
